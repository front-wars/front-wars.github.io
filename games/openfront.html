Reading the Battlefield (HUD)

Mini-map

Displays an overview of the battlefield, showing unit positions, objectives, and enemy movements. Keep an eye on it to anticipate threats.

Resource Panel

Shows your current accumulated resources. Resources are vital for building new units and structures — monitor them closely.

Unit/Building Info

When you select a unit or building, a panel provides details like health, attack power, and special abilities.

Team Score/Timer

Indicates your team's progress towards victory and the remaining match time. Adjust your strategy as the game progresses.

Core Mechanics

Army Deployment

Strategically deploy new units and construct defenses to expand your influence. Different units have unique strengths and weaknesses.

Resource Management

Resources are generated over time or collected from strategic points. Use them to upgrade your base, train advanced units, and research technologies.

Tactical Alliances

Coordinate attacks and defenses with your teammates. Sharing resources or focusing fire on key targets can turn the tide of battle.

Mastering OpenFront

Three Golden Habits

1

Proactive Reconnaissance & Adaptation

Constantly scan the battlefield, anticipate enemy movements, and adapt your army composition before a threat fully materializes. High-level players are always thinking two steps ahead.

2

Resource Cycle Optimization

Understand the precise timing of resource generation. Know when to spend aggressively for early map control, and when to conserve for a decisive late-game push.

3

Coordinated Alliance Synergy

Leverage your allies through clear communication. Set up combined pushes, provide defensive support, and execute coordinated maneuvers that overwhelm opponents through teamwork.

Elite Tactics

The "Encirclement & Starve" Maneuver

This tactic focuses on denying opponents vital resources and strategic chokepoints. Establish a layered defensive perimeter, subtly push them into an untenable position, then collapse the encirclement for a decisive victory with minimal losses. Identify a key resource node that an opponent relies on, establish an initial perimeter around it cutting off reinforcement routes, gradually tighten the noose while maintaining defensive integrity, then launch a coordinated assault from all sides.
